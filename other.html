I frequently use Maple, more specifically the q-series and subsidiary packages developed by Frank Garvan, to perform computations involving partitions and q-series. Here I keep a collection of related files where I search and find identities for partition and overpartition objects and statistics using automorphic-algorithmic techniques in Maple. Below, I have also prepared an installation guide for these packages that are available for download at Frank Garvan's UF homepage.
