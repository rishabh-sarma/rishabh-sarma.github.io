Home

Welcome to my website! I am currently an S. Chowla Research Assistant Professor at The Pennsylvania State University. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics in August, 2025 from the University of Florida, where I was fortunate to be advised by Frank Garvan. In Fall 2023, I was a Chateaubriand Fellow at IRIF, Université de Paris working under the supervision of Jeremy Lovejoy.


My area of research is additive number theory with a focus on integer partitions, q-series and modular forms. I use techniques from q-hypergeometric transformations, automorphic forms and combinatorics to look at problems in this area. See Publications

My Ph.D. thesis is titled "Modular symmetries of rank-type partition statistics".
